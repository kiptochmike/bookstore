About Us

Savani’s Book Centre located in Nairobi Kenya, is your one stop bookshop for all your primary school books need. Whether you are a Librarian, head teacher or parent wishing to stock on books so that your pupils can be well equipped for learning, we have got you covered with our affordable primary school books. We have exercise books and textbooks. We stock Textbooks from renowned publishers mandated to publish these books by the ministry of education. You can rest assured that when you buy books from us, your pupils will be getting nothing but the bets learning materials that will enable them prepare and pass their national examinations with flying colors.
